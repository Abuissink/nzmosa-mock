New Zealand Maths Olympiad Student Association


Find Out More

Welcome to the official site of the New Zealand Mathematical Olympiad Student Association!

We are a student-run student association specialising in extracurricular maths at a high school level. We publish monthly competitions, hold the New Zealand Maths Olympiad and organise lectures and tutorial materials for Maths Olympiad and other extracurricular maths.

What We Run

Monthly Problems

A set of problems released monthly, that students of all levels can enter and try and win small prizes. These are free to enter and can be found on our public website.

Learn More

NZ Maths Olympiad

Both more difficult & prestigous than the Monthly Problems, this test allows high school students to showcase their abilityS. Teachers can register their students online.

Learn More

Online Resources

We create online resources, such as documents and videos, for students and teachers looking to improve their maths skills or understand more about NZMOSA

Learn More

Student Lectures

Students in Auckland can go to free lectures hosted by NZMOSA to learn skills relevant to Maths Olympiad. More information will be announced as the year continues.
